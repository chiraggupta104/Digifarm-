Global Initiatives

Emboldened by what we’ve achieved in India and Ethiopia, Digital Green has begun a new chapter, expanding our efforts to make an even larger impact worldwide. Wherever we go, we will always keep our commitment to starting with the needs of rural communities.

DLEC Project

Funded By: USAID

Duration: 2016 - 2021

Location: Multiple

DLEC Project

The Feed the Future Developing Local Extension Capacity (DLEC) project is designed to diagnose, test and share locally-customized, best-fit solutions for agricultural extension systems and services across countries in South Asia, Latin America, and Sub-Saharan Africa.

Learn More

SAFANSI Project

Funded by: World Bank

Duration: 2016 - 2018

SAFANSI Project

South Asia Food and Nutrition Security Initiative (SAFANSI) project, funded by the World Bank, strives to improve knowledge, behavior and practices that lead to improved nutritional outcomes for the targeted communities in the Chittagong Hill Tracts in Bangladesh.

Capacity building in Malawi

Funded by: USAID

Duration: 2016 - 2017

Capacity building in Malawi

Digital Green is working with Catholic Relief Services (CRS) to deliver Extension Advisory Services (EAS) to the Ministry of Agriculture in Malawi to build the capacity of government extension agents to bring about behavioral change for increased adoption of agronomic best practices.

Sustainable Agriculture in Nepal

Funded by: USAID

Duration: 2017-2022

Sustainable Agriculture in Nepal

Digital Green is working with Winrock International to increase farming households’ resilience and livelihoods by facilitating agricultural productivity improvements and expanding access to markets and market information.
